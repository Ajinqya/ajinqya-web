# Vision

Workbench is the internal codename for Ajinkya Gajbhiye's long-term personal digital ecosystem.

The first product inside Workbench is `ajinqya.com`. In the future, Workbench may also include software tools, experiments, plugins, AI projects, and other products.

`ajinqya.com` should not feel like a portfolio. It should feel like a living product that evolves continuously: a growing archive of everything Ajinkya designs, builds, writes, thinks about, learns, creates, and shares.

## Purpose

Workbench exists to give Ajinkya a durable digital home: a place to think in public, present work with care, share useful resources, and maintain a living archive that compounds over time.

The goal is not self-promotion. The goal is to build something genuinely useful and enjoyable to explore.

## Audience

Primary audiences may include:

- Ajinkya, as the owner and long-term maintainer.
- Friends, peers, collaborators, and potential partners.
- People discovering Ajinkya through writing, projects, music, resources, experiments, or search.
- Future systems that may consume structured public content.

## Success Criteria

Workbench should feel:

- Editorial.
- Product-like.
- Timeless.
- Minimal but warm.
- Interactive where appropriate.
- Carefully crafted.
- Flexible enough to evolve for years.

Someone visiting should feel rewarded for exploring. Every visit should be able to reveal something new.
